What is Escrima?

Escrima is a Filipino martial art focused on weapon-based combat, including sticks, knives, and other improvised tools. It is known for its fast and fluid movements.

Key Principles of Escrima

  • Utilization of everyday objects as weapons
  • Rapid attack and counterattack techniques
  • Strategic movement and positioning
